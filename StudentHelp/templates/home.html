Welcome at Iset Sfax

The Student Help application is a valuable resource for ISET Sfax students, providing essential academic support. It features a user-friendly interface with access to course schedules, study materials, and assignment deadlines. The app also includes a community forum for students to collaborate and seek advice. Student Help is designed to make the educational experience more efficient and connected for all students at ISET Sfax.
